Program FAQs

What is the time commitment?

Each program is 1 month long. We recommend 15-20 hours per week, but the pace can be adjusted based on your availability.

Do I need prior experience?

Beginner-level programs (Frontend, Python) are open to everyone. Full Stack requires basic programming knowledge.

Are the programs completely remote?

Yes, all programs are 100% remote. You can learn from anywhere with an internet connection.

What happens after I complete the program?

You receive a completion certificate with a verification ID. You can add this to your portfolio and resume.
