Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to hear back?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business hours. Important matters may take slightly longer.

Can I get a refund?

Please refer to our Terms & Conditions for detailed information about refund policies or contact support@skillhub.com.

How do I report a technical issue?

Please email support@skillhub.com with details of the issue. Include screenshots if possible to help us assist you faster.

Is SkillHub a registered organization?

SkillHub is a private training initiative. We are not affiliated with any government body, university, or statutory authority.

Can corporate teams enroll?

Yes! We offer bulk enrollment options for corporate teams. Please contact info@skillhub.com for partnership inquiries.

Do you offer mentorship after program completion?

For information about post-completion support or extended mentorship, please contact support@skillhub.com.
