Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Smart Intern Hub ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information on our website.

2. Data Collection

We collect information you voluntarily provide through forms, including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Educational background
  • Program selection
  • Message content from contact forms

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information for:

  • Processing your application
  • Sending program updates and notifications
  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Improving our website and services

4. No Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is used exclusively for Smart Intern Hub operations.

5. No Cookies or Tracking

We do NOT use cookies or tracking technologies. Our website is designed to respect your privacy completely. We do not collect analytics about your browsing behavior.

6. Data Storage & Security

Any data provided through our forms is:

  • Stored securely on our servers
  • Protected with industry-standard encryption
  • Accessed only by authorized personnel
  • Never shared with external parties

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Request a copy of your submitted data
  • Opt-out of communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Contact for Privacy Concerns

For any privacy-related inquiries or requests, contact us at:

Email: privacy@smartinternhub.com

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
