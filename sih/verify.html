About Verification

Each Smart Intern Hub certificate has a unique Certificate ID that can be verified to confirm authenticity.

Certificate ID Format:

SIH-[DOMAIN]-[DATE]-[SERIAL]
  • SIH: Smart Intern Hub
  • DOMAIN: FED (Frontend), PYT (Python), FST (Full Stack)
  • DATE: YYYYMMDD format
  • SERIAL: 3-digit serial number

Example:

SIH-FED-20260110-045

Note: Verification is handled manually by Smart Intern Hub. For queries, contact us at info@smartinternhub.com
