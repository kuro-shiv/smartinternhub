What Is The SkillHub Certificate?

Upon successful completion of any SkillHub internship program, you receive a Training and Internship Completion Certificate. This certificate is a recognition of your practical skills, project work, and commitment to learning.

Each certificate includes a unique Verification ID that allows employers and others to verify your achievements. This makes your certificate a valuable addition to your portfolio and resume.

Important Information About Our Certificates

SkillHub Certificates Are Training/Internship Completion Certificates

Our certificates represent successful completion of a training program and internship experience. They are NOT:

  • Government-recognized degrees or qualifications
  • Issued or recognized by AICTE, UGC, or any educational board
  • Formal academic credentials
  • University degrees or diplomas
  • Statutory qualifications

Our certificates ARE:

  • Recognition of practical skills and project experience
  • Verifiable through our verification system
  • Valuable for building your portfolio
  • Useful for demonstrating hands-on experience to employers
  • A record of your commitment to learning

Certificate FAQs

When will I receive my certificate?

Certificates are issued within 5-7 business days after program completion and verification of all project submissions.

Can I get a physical copy of my certificate?

Certificates are primarily issued digitally. Physical copies can be requested for an additional fee. Contact us for details.

Is my certificate recognized by employers?

Yes, many employers value practical experience and project portfolios. Our certificates are verifiable and showcase your real skills.

How long is the certificate valid?

Your certificate is valid indefinitely. The skills you've learned remain relevant throughout your career.

Can I share my certificate online?

Yes, you can share your certificate on LinkedIn, your portfolio, resume, or any professional platform.

What if I don't complete the program?

You won't receive the completion certificate, but you may receive a participation certificate acknowledging your enrollment.
